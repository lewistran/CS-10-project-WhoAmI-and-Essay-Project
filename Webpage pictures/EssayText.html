1. Food

Food is what represents my identity. Pho is my favorite food to eat
because it is a type of Vietnamese Noodle Soup that
was first founded in Vietnam. It is the same as other type of noodle soups, but Pho uses rice noodles as the noodle of choice
because rice noodles are popular in Vietnam, and it has a great flavor,so good that it matches to other food and flavours.
Pho also has toppings that will make your pho taste amazing. You could order a pho that has:

  • Green and White onions.
  • The meat of choice.
  • Bean Sprouts, Basil Leaf.
  • Some Spices, Limes, Sauces
Either way, your pho will taste as good as it is
no matter what toppings you add.
